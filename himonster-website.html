About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an adrenaline-pumping survival game where your only goal is to stay alive as long as possible. Face waves of increasingly difficult monsters, collect power-ups, and upgrade your character to withstand the monster apocalypse.

With simple controls and addictive gameplay, Monster Survivors offers an exciting gaming experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat. How long can you survive?

Endless Gameplay

Face endless waves of monsters with increasing difficulty. Each game is unique and challenging, putting your survival skills to the test.

Character Upgrades

Collect power-ups and upgrade your character's abilities. Become stronger as you progress and unlock new ways to defeat monsters.

Various Enemies

Battle different types of monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns. Adapt your strategy to overcome every challenge.

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Automatically attack nearby monsters and collect experience orbs to level up. Choose new abilities and upgrades as you progress to become stronger.

Survive as long as possible and try to beat your previous record. The longer you survive, the more challenging the game becomes!
